TrainerKelly's Network

This is what I do: TrainerKelly's Network.

The best way to describe TrainerKelly's Network is an upcoming online archive of my original works with a business model.

I really love to tell stories & draw pictures and share them with people. However, I haven't really had a centralized platform that allowed me full control. For me, I want TrainerKelly's Network to be that platform: a website people can go on and view my works, all in one place.

I like to think TrainerKelly's Network is the final evolution of those websites I used to make in Microsoft Publisher back in elementary school and the mishmash-of-fandom websites in Microsoft Frontpage and Adobe Dreamweaver from middle school & high school. Even after coming up with the name, I still had difficulty describing the concept until recently.

TrainerKelly's Network is one of the reasons why I'm pursuing web development. If you're concerned that I might stop learning at some point, don't be: I have reasons to keep going beyond a job or a career. This is one of them.

Hurricane500000

Hurricane500000 (hurricane five-hundred-thousand; Hurricane500k or H500k for short) is my husband's YouTube channel that I help manage part-time and provide occasional graphics & illustrations. He is a video game channel where he films Let's Plays – video game walkthroughs with entertaining commentary – and utilizes his voice acting skills to add an extra flare to his content. As of March 2020, he has over 3,600 subscribers.

I am mostly in charge of helping him make business decisions for his channel and creating illustrations for his use in videos and channel graphics. I am the illustrator (or artist, if that's what you want to call me) between the two of us. I also have created some graphics for him, including his most recent logo.

Besides from business decisions and illustrations, I also occassionally provide voices and commentary in his videos.
